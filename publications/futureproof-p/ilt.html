Illustrative Life Table

The following mortality table follows the Makeham law (i.e., \(\mu_x=A+Bc^x\), where \(A=0.0007\), \(B=0.00005\), and \(c=10^{0.04}\)). The commutation functions \(D_x\), \(N_x\), \(C_x\), and \(M_x\) are constructed using a \(6\%\) annual effective interest rate assumption as follows:

\[ \boxed{ \begin{aligned} \\ \quad D_x &= v^xl_x \quad \\ \\ N_x &= \displaystyle \sum_{k=0}^\infty D_{x+k} \quad \\ \\ \quad C_x &= v^{x+1}d_x \quad \\ \\ M_x &= \displaystyle \sum_{k=0}^\infty C_{x+k} \quad \\ \\ \end{aligned} } \]
